About Us

Let's see

Introducing Medico Plus, your gateway to quality healthcare services in unfamiliar territories. In moments of urgency or uncertainty, finding reliable medical assistance can be daunting. Our platform revolutionizes this experience by seamlessly connecting users with experienced and available doctors, regardless of their location. Say goodbye to delays and subpar care – with Medico Plus, help is just a click away. Our user-friendly portal ensures accessibility for all, offering multilingual support to cater to diverse needs. Whether you're traveling abroad or settling in a new area, access to healthcare shouldn't be a challenge. That's why we provide each user with a personalized medical card, containing their complete medical history. This card not only streamlines appointments but also facilitates medication purchases at pharmacies.
